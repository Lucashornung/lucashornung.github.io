Welcome - I'm Lucas! I'm currently an atmospheric science PhD student at the University of California Davis. I'm originally from Houston, TX and graduated from Clear Falls High School (go Knights!) in 2022. After highschool, I attended Long Island University - Post (go Sharks!) where I played NCAA D1 tennis and pursued a dual-degree in mathematics and computer science. I had originally planned to pursue a career in electrical engineering (following in my father's footsteps), but after spending a summer volunteering for the Student Conservation Assocation in the backcountry wilderness of Washington state, I knew I wanted to be an Earth scientist. From there, I interned for two summers in the Cloud Processes Group at Brookhaven National Laboratory, fell in love with cloud physics, and the rest is history!
